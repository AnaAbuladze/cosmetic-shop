ABOUT US

MINERAL MAKEUP · PURE BEAUTY

Creating beauty through minerals

Since 2018, Noelle has redefined makeup with mineral-based formulas that deliver stunning color and flawless finish. We craft lipsticks, eyeshadows, and foundations that perform beautifully.

Every product is developed by cosmetic scientists who believe luxury makeup can be pure, powerful, and transformative. From bold lips to perfect coverage, we create makeup that makes you feel unstoppable.

OUR MINERALS

01

Mica

Creates luminous glow in highlighters and eyeshadows. Natural shimmer that catches light beautifully.

02

Iron Oxides

Rich pigmentation for lipsticks, blushes, and eyeshadows. True-to-tone color that lasts.

03

Titanium Dioxide

Creates smooth, even coverage in foundations. Natural sun protection with flawless finish.

04

Zinc Oxide

Foundation base that soothes while providing coverage. Balances and protects skin.

05

Silica

Soft-focus effect in powders. Blurs imperfections and controls shine effortlessly.

06

Kaolin Clay

Oil control in foundations and face products. Keeps makeup fresh and matte all day.

Lipsticks, eyeshadows & foundations

Our lipsticks deliver bold, long-lasting color with comfortable wear. From matte reds to glossy nudes, every shade is infused with nourishing ingredients.

Eyeshadow palettes offer versatile shades that blend seamlessly—from everyday neutrals to dramatic evening looks. Cream-to-powder formulas that stay vibrant.

Foundations provide buildable coverage that looks like skin, not makeup. Lightweight formulas that perfect your complexion while letting your natural beauty shine.

OUR CRAFT

INGREDIENTS

Vitamin E

Antioxidant-rich moisturization in lipsticks and liquid foundations. Protects lips and skin while delivering beautiful color.

Jojoba Oil

Smooth, comfortable wear in all lip products. Mimics natural skin oils for balanced, non-drying formulas.

Hyaluronic Acid

Moisture-locking technology in foundations and primers. Keeps makeup looking fresh while hydrating skin.

Chamomile Extract

Calming properties for sensitive skin. Natural anti-inflammatory benefits in face makeup formulas.

Green Tea Extract

Antioxidant protection in foundations and primers. Shields skin from environmental stress throughout wear.

Aloe Vera

Soothing hydration across all product lines. Maintains moisture balance for comfortable all-day wear.
